Summary

Myrstar is the medium character. He is a quirky character who acts as a character foil to Shield Hero, choosing to think before acting instead of rushing a decision.

Attributes

He has increased Wisdom and Intelligence, lowered Defence and Wisdom. Tool mastery is gained through casting.

Types

Darkness and Arcane are his types.
