Summary

The main character is Shield Hero. The player can keep track of different stats, found HERE.

Attributes

Attributes are Strength, Intelligence, Wisdom, Agility, Shield Mastery, Assault Shield Mastery.

Types

Types are Fire, Ice/Water, Air, Earth, Magic.

Skills

Skill Description
Shield Bash Use your shield to slam into the enemy.
Block Defend yourself by using your shield to block attacks (has a block meter).
Shield Charge Charge in with your shield damaging the first enemy you hit (Head down, Shield forward).
Shield Spam A weaker version of Shield Bash but it has a higher attack rate.
Salty Shield Spam Infuses Shield Spam with some salt to increase its damage and attack speed.
Shield Counter Use the enemies power against them and protect your self while doing so.
Salty Shield Counter Infuses Shield Counter with some salt to be able to make more use of the enemies attacking power.
Shield Aura Boosts nearby teammates defence levels.
Shield Throw Throw your shield at the enemy for a ranged attack (during shield time in air you are vulnerable).
Guard You have found a way to defend yourself and attack at the same time reducing damage taken for a short while.
Victory Shout After killing an enemy with a Salty move Shield Hero will shout in victory temporarily boosting attack power.
Deflect You have discovered a way to use the enemies arrows against them.
Shield Finisher You infuse all your rage and saltiness and repeatedly smash the enemy relentlessly even if they are dead for a short while and you somehow are able to ignore all damage in this phase.
